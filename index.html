❖ Web Application Project: Project on University Management System

This application is split-up in three interfaces where:

1. University Administration able to manage students, teachers, staffs, subject and assign courses to the subject, producing reports, pay salary to all staffs, generate semester wise fees, residences, vehicles management etc.
